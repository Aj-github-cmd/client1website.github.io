PRE-CARE

Prior to your treatment think about the look that you wish to achieve. As experts in the field of colour analysis and brow transformations, we ensure that the correct colours and styles are chosen for you, however, you are a part of the decision-making process. Semi-permanent cosmetic tattoos normally require multiple application sessions to achieve the best and desired results. You will be required to return for at least one perfecting procedure 4-6 weeks after the initial procedure. Be prepared for the colour intensity to be significantly sharper and darker immediately after the procedure. This will subside and become softer as the skin heals. This process can take up to fourteen days.

FOLLOW THESE INSTRUCTIONS
BEFORE YOUR TREATMENT:

  • Do not take aspirin or panadol 48 hours before your appointment
  • Do not drink alcohol for 48 hours before
  • No retin-A skincare products on area to be treated for 2 weeks prior and after procedure
  • Do not use AHA skincare products on area to be treated for 1 month prior and after your procedure
  • No facials, peels or laser treatments on area to be treated for one month prior and after procedure
  • No giving blood for 12 months after treatment
  • Waxing treatments to be done no less than 3 days prior
  • No eyebrow tint for 2 weeks prior
  • No botox or filler procedures for 2 weeks prior or after treatment

AFTER-CARE

Once leaving the salon, it is important to follow all aftercare steps religiously to ensure your brows heal properly. They take between 4-6 weeks to heal and caring for them properly will leave you with the best possible results. You are required to return for a 4-6 week perfecting session after your initial procedure. Be prepared for the colour intensity to be significantly sharper and darker immediately after the procedure. This will subside and become softer as the skin heals. This process can take up to fourteen days.

AFTERCARE INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Skin may be sensitive, slightly swollen or red, so avoid major social plans on day of appointment
  • No makeup on brow area for 2 weeks
  • No exercise or excessive sweating for 2 weeks
