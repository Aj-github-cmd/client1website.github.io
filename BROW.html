BROWS

EYEBROW WAX + RESHAPE

Brows are the most important feature on your face. Our experienced brow artists will discuss your brow goals with you, then measure and draw up your brows. Once you are happy with the shape they will perform a wax, leaving you with perfect brows to suit your face.

Our Eyebrow Wax + Reshape service takes roughly twenty minutes.

HD BROWS

Give your brows the 3D effect. We are able to achieve this through tinting. We use a colour that matches your natural brow, giving you a more defined and fuller brow. This service also includes a wax + reshape and lasts between two to three weeks.

You won’t need to powder or fill in your brows!

Our HD Brow service takes between thirty to forty five minutes.

HENNA BROWS

Step up your brow game with a more intense, longer lasting tint. The tint is left on longer at the tail of your brow and less at the beginning of your brow to achieve an ombre look. The henna stains the skin and is a great substitute for cosmetic tattooing. This service includes a wax + reshape and lasts usually three weeks.

You won’t need to powder or fill in your brows!

Our Henna Brow service takes between thirty five to forty five minutes.

HD LAMINATION BROWS

We straighten your brows in an upwards directions using a keratin solution. This leaves you with a fuller and glossy look, very similar to that of feathering. This service also includes a wax + reshape and a tint.

Our HD Lamination Brow service takes fifty minutes to an hour.

MICROSHADING

A technique that gives a natural yet airbrushed ombre looking result.

The perfect eyebrow treatment choice for those with oily skin and larger pores.

This procedure is done with a semi permanent machine, a cluster of needles which inserts pigment into the epidermis and gives the effect of an airbrushed look.

Microshading takes two and a half hours to complete.

BLADE + SHADE

A combination of Microblading and Microshading, a 3D yet airbrushed finish. This procedure is quite popular as it combines both Blading and Shading leaving a flawless look for our clients.

Blade + Shade takes two and a half hours to complete.
