LASHES

KERATIN LASH LIFT AND TINT

Move away from using the curling wand every morning with the LT Keratin Lash Lift and Tint. A natural, and low maintenance substitute to Lash Extensions.

This treatment acts as an instant root lift for your natural lashes, whilst the keratin achieves a flourishing and durable coat to nourish your lashes. We usually recommend our clients come in every six weeks for this service.

Our Keratin Lash Lift and Tint takes roughly fifty minutes to an hour.

LASH EXTENSIONS

Binding extensions onto your natural lashes to give a fuller, more intense lash.

Infills are recommended every 2-3 weeks.

Our Lash Extensions take anywhere between an hour and a half to two hours.
